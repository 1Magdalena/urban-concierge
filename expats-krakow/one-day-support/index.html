One Day Support by Urban Concierge is a one-time concierge service in Kraków and surrounding areas. We help with everyday organisation: calls, bookings, pickups, returns, research, contacting service providers, appointment coordination and follow-ups. The service is available without subscription, as a Small Package or Large Package.

Not everything requires ongoing cooperation.

Sometimes one well-organised day is enough to close several pending tasks, reduce mental load and bring order back to things that keep coming back.

What One Day Support includes

This is practical help with getting things done in Kraków, nearby areas or remotely — especially when the tasks are not complicated, but require time, calls, arrangements and attention to detail.

Everyday tasks

  • booking an appointment, service or consultation
  • confirming delivery, assembly or pickup times
  • contacting a service provider, building administration or institution
  • organising a gift, flowers, reservation or transport
  • following up on something that needs to be pushed forward

Coordination tasks

  • finding and comparing several service providers or options
  • arranging times between several people
  • coordinating a repair, visit, pickup or formal process
  • checking availability and gathering information
  • organising several pending items from one list

Urban Concierge does not replace a doctor, lawyer, accountant, tax advisor or any other licensed specialist. We help with organisation, coordination, communication and process follow-up.
