What One-Day Support includes

Support with matters in Krakow, nearby areas or remotely — especially when tasks are not difficult, but require time, calls, arrangements and attention to detail.

Everyday matters

  • booking an appointment, service or consultation
  • confirming delivery, installation or pickup time
  • contact with a service provider, administration office or institution
  • organising a gift, flowers, booking or transport
  • following up on a matter that requires attention

Coordination tasks

  • finding and comparing several provider or service options
  • arranging dates between several people
  • coordination of a repair, appointment, pickup or formalities
  • checking availability and gathering information
  • organising several delayed matters from one list

Urban Concierge does not replace a doctor, lawyer, accountant, tax adviser or any other licensed specialist. We help with organisation, coordination, communication and process follow-up.
