One-Day Support is the simplest way to use concierge services without ongoing cooperation. You send the tasks, we confirm the scope and coordinate them on the agreed day.

What One-Day Support includes

Help with everyday matters in Krakow, nearby areas or remotely — especially when the tasks are not difficult, but require time, calls, arrangements and attention to detail.
Everyday matters
  • booking an appointment, service or consultation
  • confirming a delivery, installation or pick-up time
  • contacting a provider, administration office or facility
  • arranging a gift, flowers, reservation or transport
  • following up on a matter that has been left open
Matters that need coordination
  • finding and comparing several service options
  • coordinating dates between several people
  • organizing a repair, visit, pick-up or practical formality
  • checking availability and gathering information
  • bringing several delayed tasks into order
If a matter requires specialist knowledge, a license or your final decision, Urban Concierge can support the organization of the process, but does not replace a licensed professional.
