What relocation support may include

The scope depends on your situation, timeline and what you want to delegate for coordination. Support may cover preparation before arrival, the first days in Kraków and practical matters after the move.

Before arrival

  • initial plan of things to handle
  • coordination of selected appointments
  • preparation of a first-step list
  • contact with selected service providers
  • plan for local matters during the first days

Housing and local logistics

  • coordination of deliveries and access
  • internet, utilities and basic services
  • selected pickups or handovers
  • local handling of practical matters
  • housing-related follow-ups

First matters in Poland

  • support with PESEL, if applicable
  • support with registration of address, if applicable
  • support with bank account setup, if applicable
  • coordination of documents and appointments
  • practical help for foreigners in Kraków

Urban Concierge supports organisation, coordination and practical handling of selected matters. We do not replace a lawyer, tax advisor, public office, bank or any other licensed specialist.
