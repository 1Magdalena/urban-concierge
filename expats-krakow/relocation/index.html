What this service is

Relocation to Kraków is a dedicated support service for individuals, couples and families who want one trusted person to coordinate key practical matters before arrival and during the first stage of settling in.
Before arrival

Preparation, coordination and local setup so that your first days in Kraków are not spent chasing utilities, appointments, service providers or missing essentials.

After arrival

Practical on-the-ground support with the early stage of settling in — the matters that still need local handling, decisions, presence or follow-through.

What this may include

A calmer move, from the practical side.
The exact scope depends on your situation, timing and what you want delegated. This may include:
  • arrival preparation and local planning before you move
  • apartment setup before arrival
  • coordination of utilities, internet and selected services
  • arranging practical appointments and local follow-ups
  • settling-in support during the first stage in Kraków
  • coordination with a trusted real estate partner when needed
  • selected support in Silesia available on request
