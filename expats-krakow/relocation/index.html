What it may include

The exact scope depends on your situation, timing and what you want to delegate. Relocation support may include:

Before arrival

  • preparing the apartment before arrival
  • coordinating internet and selected utilities
  • coordinating deliveries, access and basic apartment readiness
  • selected move-in arrangements
  • planning local matters for the first days after arrival

After arrival

  • local coordination on site
  • booking selected appointments
  • practical support after moving in
  • follow-ups related to the apartment and daily life
  • organizing the first stage after relocation

First formalities

  • support with PESEL, if applicable
  • support with address registration, if applicable
  • support with opening a bank account, if applicable
  • practical support for foreigners in Krakow
  • local support during the first weeks after arrival

Urban Concierge supports organization, coordination and practical follow-through. We do not replace a lawyer, tax advisor, public office, bank or any licensed professional.

Yes. Support may begin before arrival in Poland and continue during the first stage after you arrive.

Yes. Selected practical matters may be coordinated before arrival, depending on timing and scope.

Can you help prepare the apartment?

Yes. Selected apartment preparation elements may be part of relocation support if they are needed in your situation.

Can support include internet, utilities and appointments?

Yes. Depending on the scope, we can coordinate selected practical matters such as internet, utilities or selected appointments.

Is this service only for expats?

No. It may also be suitable for international clients, couples, families and people moving to Krakow from other places.

Is support available only in Krakow?

The main area of operation is Krakow. Selected support outside Krakow may be possible depending on the situation.
