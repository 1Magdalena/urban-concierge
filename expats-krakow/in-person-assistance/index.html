When to use in-person assistance

In-person assistance is the right option when remote organisation is not enough — presence, punctuality, communication and calm supervision are needed.

Presence on site

For matters where someone needs to be physically present: during an appointment, pickup, formality, handover, meeting or situation that requires supervision.

Less operational pressure

You do not have to manage everything in a rush. Assistance brings order to the process, confirmations, presence and communication where real support is needed.
