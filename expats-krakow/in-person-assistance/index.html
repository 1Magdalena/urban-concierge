FAQ

Does assistance mean unlimited care without boundaries?

No. The scope is always confirmed specifically in advance, so the situation is handled calmly, realistically and within the right boundaries.

Can assistance be provided for a close family member?

Yes, in selected situations. The exact form and scope are always confirmed beforehand, so everything is clear and properly matched.

Can you assist during formalities or selected visits?

Yes. This is one of the most natural scenarios for this service, especially when presence, confirmations and calm coordination are needed.

Can assistance include transport?

Yes, depending on scope. In selected situations, assistance may also include transport or moving between locations within the agreed flow.

Do you operate only in Kraków?

Kraków and nearby are the main area of operation. Selected assistance outside this area may be possible depending on the situation and timing.

Can I start with one single assistance request?

Yes. This is often the best first step when the client wants to test the collaboration style in one specific situation requiring presence.

Can assistance be part of a broader collaboration?

Yes. Depending on the situation, it may stand alone or form part of a broader model, such as One Support Day or Monthly Support.

How is pricing calculated?

Pricing depends on location, duration, complexity, required level of presence, transport and whether prior arrangements or further follow-up are needed.
