What monthly support may include

Monthly support is for matters that return regularly and take attention: someone needs to call, follow up, book, compare, pick up, confirm or return to a topic after a few days.

Ongoing coordination

  • coordination of selected matters in the background
  • contact with service providers within the agreed scope
  • confirmations, follow-ups and next-step tracking
  • organising current operational topics
  • handling matters through one point of contact

Appointments and arrangements

  • booking selected appointments
  • reservations and confirmations
  • coordination of meetings and arrangements
  • reminders and important date tracking
  • organising selected calendar-related matters

Local and remote support

  • remote coordination across Poland
  • selected actions on site in Krakow and nearby areas
  • local completion of matters requiring presence
  • coordination of selected pickups or access
  • combining organisational support with local action
