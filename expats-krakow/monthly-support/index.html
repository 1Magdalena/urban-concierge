Monthly Support is an ongoing concierge arrangement for clients who want one trusted person to coordinate recurring matters, handle practical follow-ups and reduce the amount of personal admin still sitting on their head.
Ongoing, not one-off

This service is designed for situations where support is needed regularly, not just once. The value comes from continuity, context and someone who already knows how things need to be handled.

Trusted coordination
