No subscription. When you need it and for what you need.
Urban Concierge is practical support for people who have too much on their plate and want to delegate part of their everyday logistics to someone who can calmly organise it.

What you can delegate

It works best for concrete matters: something needs to be found, ordered, picked up, returned, booked, confirmed or followed up.
City errands
  • pickup or return of items
  • shopping and small local errands
  • document or package handover
  • coordination of pickup, delivery or appointment
  • handling several points from one list
Organisational matters
  • calls and appointment confirmations
  • bookings and scheduling appointments
  • research and offer comparison
  • contact with service providers
  • follow-up on matters that got stuck
