What you can hand over

One clear list of specific matters works best. You explain what needs to be handled. We confirm the scope and take over the organisation.
Everyday matters
  • arranging an appointment, service or consultation
  • confirming a delivery, installation or collection time
  • contacting a service provider, administration or institution
  • organising a gift, flowers, booking or transport
  • following up on a matter that needs attention
Matters requiring coordination
  • finding and comparing several service options
  • aligning timings between several people
  • coordinating a repair, visit, collection or formality
  • checking availability and gathering practical information
  • bringing order to several delayed matters from one list
If a matter requires specialist expertise, a licence or a decision on your side, Urban Concierge can support the organisation of the process, but does not replace a specialist.
