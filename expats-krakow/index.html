Service Models

Urban Concierge works in several formats — depending on whether you need one specific day of support, in-person presence, ongoing support or help with relocation.

This is a structured way to begin working together for clients who want to hand over selected matters for coordination without starting with ongoing monthly support. The client may send a list of topics in advance, the scope is confirmed before the start, and on the agreed day their matters receive priority.
