Urban Concierge is a local concierge service in Kraków for people who want to delegate part of their everyday organisation. We help with errands, calls, reservations, pick-ups, returns, shopping, research, comparing offers, follow-ups and simple coordination tasks. The service works without a subscription — you can book a specific hourly slot.
No subscription. Only when you need it.
Urban Concierge is practical support for people who have too many small tasks in the background and want to hand over part of their daily logistics to someone who can organise, follow up and close the loop.

What you can delegate

The service works best for clear, practical tasks: something needs to be found, booked, picked up, returned, confirmed, compared or followed up.
Local errands in Kraków
  • pick-ups and returns
  • shopping and small local tasks
  • delivering documents or parcels
  • coordinating collections, deliveries or appointments
  • handling several stops from one task list
Organisation and coordination
  • calls and appointment confirmations
  • reservations and bookings
  • research and comparing offers
  • contacting service providers
  • following up on issues that got stuck
Local errands are handled mainly in Kraków and nearby areas. Remote tasks — calls, research, bookings, comparing offers and coordination — can be handled from anywhere in Poland.

FAQ

What does Urban Concierge do?

We help with everyday tasks: calls, reservations, pick-ups, returns, shopping, research, comparing offers, local errands and coordination of things the client wants to delegate.

Do I need a subscription?

No. You can book a single hourly slot. The minimum online booking is 2 hours.

Do you only work in Kraków?

Local errands and personal assistance are handled mainly in Kraków and nearby areas. Remote tasks such as calls, research, reservations and coordination can be handled from anywhere in Poland.

Are shopping costs or external fees included?

No. The price covers Urban Concierge working time. External costs such as shopping, tickets, transport, payments or service provider fees are covered separately by the client.
